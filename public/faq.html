Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are your library hours?

Our library is open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

How can I borrow books from your library?

To borrow books, you need to have a valid library card. You can apply for a library card at our circulation desk by presenting a valid ID.

Do you offer digital resources?

Yes, we provide access to a wide range of digital resources including eBooks, audiobooks, online databases, and more. These resources can be accessed through our website with your library account.

How can I renew my borrowed books?

You can renew your borrowed books online through your library account on our website, or by visiting our circulation desk in person.
